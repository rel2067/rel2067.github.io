    About LightningRoundsAI

    LightningRoundsAI was started by two pre-medical students at the University of Miami who believe that access to medical literature shouldn't be limited to those who already speak the language of medicine.

    Our mission is simple: expand access to medical literature by translating complex studies into clear, tiered summaries that meet readers where they are — whether you're a curious patient, a pre-med student, or a clinician reviewing your own field.

    How It Works

    1

    We curate the research

    We monitor high-impact journals — NEJM, Lancet, JAMA, BMJ, and more — selecting studies that matter clinically.

    2

    AI drafts the summaries

    Our AI generates three-level summaries tuned to different knowledge levels, with clinical context and key points.

    3

    We review & verify

    Every summary is reviewed before publication to ensure accuracy and clarity.
