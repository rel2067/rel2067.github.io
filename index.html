About MinuteMed.ai

MinuteMed.ai was born in a study room at the University of Miami. Ryan Lee and Trevor Spinelli, two pre-medical undergraduates, kept running into the same frustration: groundbreaking medical research was being published every week, but understanding it required years of specialized training.

Friends and family would ask about studies they'd seen in the news — and even as pre-med students, explaining them clearly was a challenge. MinuteMed.ai exists to bridge that gap. Every article is explained at three levels so that everyone from a curious patient to a practicing clinician can engage with the same research.

Our commitment: We only publish summaries for articles we can independently verify. Every article links to the original source. We never fabricate author names, journal names, or study findings. If we cannot verify a detail, we do not publish it.

How It Works

1

Source Verification First

We identify articles from top-tier journals and verify every detail — title, authors, journal, and findings — against the primary source before writing a single word.

2

Three-Level Summaries

Each article is summarized three ways. Beginner for anyone. Intermediate for clinical familiarity. Pro for researchers who want methodology and statistics.

3

Direct Source Links

Every article links to the original journal page. We are a starting point, not a replacement for the primary research.
